# (A More) Objective View of AI

These *open-source* notes aim at offering a more *balanced* and *objective* view of Artificial Intelligence (AI).

The profound influence of AI on technology, human culture, and society is undeniable.
The landscape of opinions about AI ranges from predictions of imminent societal or economic collapse to people being overexcited and overly reliant on it at the opposite extreme.

*Audience*: 

- People concerned about the impact of AI and want to know more from a scientific point of view (as me).
- People that anthropomorphize AI or believe it is conscious (it is not!).
- Informational technology workers overexcited and overly reliant on AI and Large Language Models (LLMs).
